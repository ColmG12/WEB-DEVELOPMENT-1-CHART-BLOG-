Colm G - Welcome!

Hello!

My name is Colm Gallagher. I have loved charts since I was a young boy. Back in the early 1990's I would listen every Sunday afternoon to the UK top 40 and even wrote them all down in a copy book to rate the songs every week (I know I'm a nerd!)

This passion has stayed with me ever since and has also played a vital part in my professional life too as a DJ and radio presenter. But you can learn more on my life here About Me

I plan on producing as much info about the charts as I can on this site, and you can also find my all time rankings over the years since the official UK chart began in 1952. It would be great to hear from you and get both your own rankings, and your thoughts on mine too. My social media contacts are at the foot of the page

My other passion is radio, and you will be seeing lots about it on the site. Again, share your thoughts and if you enjoy visiting, please share it around the usual places!

Thanks!
